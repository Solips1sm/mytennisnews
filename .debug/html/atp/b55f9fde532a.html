Fritz outhit by Diallo, but leads American charge into Tokyo R2

Korda, Brooksby advance; Machac falls to Shimabukuro at ATP 500 event
September 25, 2025
Koji Watanabe/Getty Images
Taylor Fritz improves to 3-0 in his Lexus ATP Head2Head series against Gabriel Diallo. By Jerome Coombe

Taylor Fritz narrowly escaped an opening-round upset on Thursday at the Kinoshita Group Japan Open Tennis Championships in Tokyo, where he rallied past Gabriel Diallo 4-6, 6-3, 7-6(3).

Just days removed from his standout Laver Cup wins over Carlos Alcaraz and Alexander Zverev, Fritz found himself pushed onto the defensive against fellow big server Diallo, who struck 16 more winners (40-24).
